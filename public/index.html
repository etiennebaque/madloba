What is Madloba?

A sharing platform

Madloba allows you to let your community know about things you’re giving away, and also things you’re looking for.

A free and open-source solution

Download Madloba and use it for free! It has been released under the GNU General Public License (V2). Use our installation guide on Github to get started.

Contribute

Madloba is a very young project! If you want to participate in the growth of the Madloba project, feel free to give us a shout!

Give it a try!

Before creating your own Madloba website, how about giving it a try?
Head over to our Madloba demo site, and see what it is all about. You can find instructions here.
